HSBC Bank has announced a package of support for small businesses including access to cheaper finance.

A YouGov survey of 1,057 businesses employing 1-2 people questioned respondents on the everyday barriers that prevented them from growing. These included:

  • Too much time taken up with admin (28 per cent)

  • Managing communications with customers (17 per cent)

  • Traveling to meetings (11 per cent)

  • Tax/National Insurance (9 per cent)
